01 / ABOUT THE NIGHT
About Open Mic
This isn't built to be loud. It's built to be close.

You won't know the full lineup until you're in the room. One name on the bill is being kept sealed a performer confirmed, identity withheld, revealed only when they stand up to go. Something to genuinely look forward to, not just a name on a flyer.

Bring a piece, bring nothing, just come and listen. Both are welcome here.

  • The Dedication Draw - Upon entry, every guest writes one word or a name on a small card and drops it in a bowl. Performers pull a card before their set and dedicate/reference it in what they perform.
  • Text To Stage - audience can text/DM a prompt during the night, and one performer has to respond to it live
  • One "Mystery Seat" - a specific seat in the room comes with an unknown perk, nobody knows till the night which seat it is.
