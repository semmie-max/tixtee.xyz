WHAT

We are redefining how people discover, create, and experience events. We provide a modern ticketing platform designed for organizers who want powerful tools without unnecessary complexity, and for attendees who simply want a fast, secure, and enjoyable booking and checkout experience.

Whether you're hosting a campus event, concert, conference, workshop, festival, or community gathering, Tixtee gives you the tools to create events, sell tickets, manage attendees, and grow your audience in one place.

Our mission is to remove the stress from event management by providing a secure, reliable, and user-friendly platform that works for everyone. From seamless online ticket purchases to real-time event management, every feature is designed to help events succeed.
