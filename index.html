What is Tixtee?

Built for creators. Designed for attendees.

Tixtee is a modern ticketing platform designed around the people who make events happen. We’re building simple yet powerful tools that help creators sell, manage, and grow their events while giving attendees a seamless way to discover and join experiences they care about.
