What is Tixtee?

Built for creators. Designed for attendees.

Tixtee is a modern ticketing platform designed around the people who make events happen. We’re building simple yet powerful tools that help creators sell, manage, and grow their events while giving attendees a seamless way to discover and join experiences they care about.

EVERYTHING INCLUDED.

Built for everyone in the show.

EVENT COMMUNITY

Your event deserves more than a ticket link. Create a community, keep your audience engaged, share updates, and turn attendees into part of the experience all inside Tixtee.

GROUP DISCOUNTS

Moments are better when shared. Invite your friends, unlock group discounts, split the cost equally, and secure everyone’s ticket together without the stress of collecting payments separately.

CREATOR RATINGS

Rate your experience and explore creator ratings before buying a ticket. Make informed choices, avoid disappointments, and attend events with confidence.

EVENT REVIEWS

Creators, organizers, and attendees can share stories, reviews, and experiences by creating blogs and tagging event creators. This helps build trust, reduce fraud, and give the community more confidence in the events they choose to attend.

SMART ENTRY

Your ticket becomes your key. When you arrive, generate a secure one-time code in seconds, verify your entry, and step into the experience without the usual gate stress.

EARN AND GROW

Earn cashbacks from your event activities and use your rewards to power your next event. Keep creating, keep growing, and let every event take you further.
