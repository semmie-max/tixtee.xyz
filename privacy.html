Privacy Policy

What we collect

When you sign up, we collect your name and email address. If you sign up with Google, we receive the name and email associated with your Google account.

How we use it

We use your information to create your account, let event creators contact you about events you've shown interest in, and to notify you when new features (like your dashboard) become available.

We don't sell your data to third parties. We don't share your email with event creators unless you've bought a ticket to their event.
